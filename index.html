About Coakley's Pub

Coakley's Pub is a casual, family-friendly restaurant in the heart of Havre de Grace, just steps from the river and the shops.

The menu mixes Irish favorites with American pub food and local Maryland seafood - from steaks and crab cakes to wings and nachos.

Whether you're stopping in for a pint of Guinness, a game on TV, or a quiet dinner, there's a spot for you.

Special Events & Catering

Coakley's is available for private events like birthdays, weddings, business lunches, and more. The team can help plan food, drinks, and setup.

For catering questions or to talk about your event, please reach out by phone or email.
